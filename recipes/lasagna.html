Mama's Lasagna



Before You Make

This right here just so happens to be the most elegant, profound, nothing short of amazing recipe for the one and only "Mama's lasagna". Once youve finished all the steps you should be finding yourself overfilled with joy as well as lots of lasgna! The recipe itself is based off of my mothers old school tactics of masterfully putting together a variety of ingriedients, from start to finish this recipe will have you covered so don't you fret!

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion
  • tomato paste
  • sugar
  • spices and seasonings
  • lasgna noodles
  • cheeses
  • egg

Directions

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in oven over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomatoe paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.


    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tbsp parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt.

  2. Preheat oven to 376 degrees F(190 degrees C).


  3. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the riccota cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil or make sure foil does not touch the cheese.


  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Rest lasgna for 15 minutes before serving.
